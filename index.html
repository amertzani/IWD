Panel Discussion

This Panel Discussion is a part of series of events organised by the IEEE Student Branch and EEE Department, in celebration of the IWD 2022. This event focuses on recognising achievements of women in our department and those that support them. The panel will consist of four postgraduate students and/or professors. Each panelist will present their own technical work, the work of another woman in the department, and/or their personal stories.
On Wednesday, 9 March 2022, 11:00-13:00, the celebration continues with an event that aims to facilitate a conversation on the topic of Women in STEM through a roundtable discussion and audience participation.
